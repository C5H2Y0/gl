Exploration Tips

  • Bring enough water and food to avoid thirst and hunger in the desert
  • Watch for weather changes - sandstorms affect visibility and movement speed
  • Use map markers to record important locations
  • Explore in groups for safety - watch each other's backs
  • Look for hidden entrances and secret passages

Recommended Levels by Region

  • Oasis Town: Level 1-10
  • Yellow Sand Desert: Level 10-20
  • Flame Caves: Level 20-30
  • Lost Temple: Level 30-40
  • Storm Sea: Level 40-50
  • Crystal Desert: Level 50+
