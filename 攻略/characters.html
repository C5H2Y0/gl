Characters

Class Selection

Red Desert offers multiple classes for players to choose from. Each class has unique skills and playstyles:

🛡️

Warrior

Melee / Tank

Heavy armor and massive sword. The backbone of any team with strong defense and area attacks.

🔮

Mage

Ranged / DPS

Master of elemental magic. Deals devastating area damage but has low defense.

🏹

Ranger

Ranged / Agile

Expert with bows and traps. Excellent mobility and critical strike ability.

🗡️

Assassin

Melee / Burst

Lurks in shadows. Specializes in assassination and quick strikes for instant kills.

✨

Cleric

Support / Healer

User of divine magic. Provides healing and buffs for the team.

🐉

Summoner

Ranged / Summon

Can summon desert elementals to fight. Unique pet system and crowd control abilities.

Character Development Tips

Each class has different development paths. Here are some general tips:

  • Warrior: Prioritize Strength and Stamina
  • Mage: Prioritize Intelligence and Spirit
  • Ranger: Prioritize Agility and Critical Rate
  • Assassin: Prioritize Agility and Attack Speed
  • Cleric: Prioritize Spirit and Intelligence
  • Summoner: Balance Intelligence and Spirit
