Equipment

Weapon Types

There are multiple weapon types in the game, each with unique attributes and suitable classes:

⚔️

Greatsword

Rarity: Legendary

Two-handed weapon with the highest base attack. Ideal for Warriors.

🔮

Staff

Rarity: Epic

Range weapon that enhances magic attack. Preferred by Mages and Summoners.

🏹

Longbow

Rarity: Rare

Range weapon with long attack distance. Suitable for Rangers.

🗡️

Daggers

Rarity: Epic

Melee weapon with fast attack speed and high crit rate. Assassin's exclusive weapon.

✨

Rod

Rarity: Rare

Support weapon that enhances healing. Suitable for Clerics.

🗡️

Scimitar

Rarity: Fine

Balanced weapon with good attack and speed. Suitable for multiple classes.

Equipment Rarity

  • Common (White): Basic stats
  • Fine (Green): Slightly enhanced stats
  • Rare (Blue): Noticeably enhanced stats
  • Epic (Purple): Greatly enhanced stats with special effects
  • Legendary (Orange): Top-tier gear with powerful unique skills

Equipment Acquisition

  • Dungeon Drops: Challenge dungeons for a chance at high-quality gear
  • Quest Rewards: Complete specific quests for gear rewards
  • Shop Purchase: Buy with gold or reputation
  • Crafting: Collect materials to craft gear
  • World BOSS: Defeat world BOSS for guaranteed high-quality gear

Equipment Enhancement

Equipment can be enhanced to improve stats:

  • Enhancement Level: Up to +15 with success rate
  • Gem Socketing: Different gems provide different stat bonuses
  • Reforging: Randomly refresh gear bonus stats
  • Upgrade: Increase gear rarity level
